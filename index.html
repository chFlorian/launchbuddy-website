Frequently Asked Questions

Is LaunchBuddy free?
Yes, LaunchBuddy is free to download and use. There are additional pro features available via subscription.
Is the iOS app included if I subscribe on the Mac?
Yes, your subscription covers both the macOS and iOS versions of LaunchBuddy.
Can I suggest features?
Yes, you can suggest features via the roadmap directly in LaunchBuddy.
Does my data sync between iOS and macOS?
Yes, all your data syncs seamlessly between devices via iCloud.
